Features

Everything you need to stay on budget

Budgeter covers the full picture — from bills due this month to long-term spending patterns.

Monthly bill tracking

List every bill for the month, mark them paid as you go, and copy last month's bills in one click to save setup time.

Category budgets

Set a spending limit per category. As transactions come in, a progress bar shows exactly how close you are to the edge.

Bank CSV import

Supports exports from Bank of America, Capital One, Citi, and more. Transactions are deduplicated automatically on re-import.

Smart categorization rules

Create keyword rules that auto-assign categories — "Spotify" → Entertainment, "Trader Joe's" → Groceries — so categorizing is mostly hands-off.

Spending analytics

See a monthly breakdown by category and merchant, track trends over time, and get flagged when a transaction looks unusually large.

Receipt scanning

Snap a photo of a receipt and Budgeter uses AI to extract the merchant, date, total, and line items automatically.
